Courses
Qaida with Tajweed

This course is designed to teach students the fundamental principles of reading Arabic with correct pronunciation Tajweed. It focuses on the basic rules of phonetics and articulation to ensure accurate recitation of the Quran.

Duration: 8 weeks

Nazra Quran Pak

The Nazra course emphasizes the reading of the Quran with proper articulation and fluency. Students will learn to read the Quran accurately, focusing on correct pronunciation and comprehension of the verses.

Duration: 8 weeks

Hifz ul Quran

This course is dedicated to memorizing the Quran. Students will be guided through a structured plan to help them commit verses to memory while ensuring accurate recitation and understanding.

Duration: 8 weeks

Farz Uloom

Farz Uloom covers the essential Islamic knowledge that every Muslim must know, including beliefs, practices, and moral values. This course aims to strengthen the foundational understanding of Islamic teachings.

Duration: 8 weeks

Tafseer e Quran

In this course, students will explore the meanings and interpretations of the Quranic verses. Emphasis will be placed on understanding the context, themes, and lessons derived from the Quran.

Duration: 8 weeks

Dars e Nizami (Short)

This short course provides an introduction to the Dars-e-Nizami curriculum, which encompasses various Islamic sciences. It offers students a glimpse into classical Islamic education and foundational studies.

Duration: 8 weeks

Basic Islamic Information Course

Tailored for children and newly converted Muslims, this course covers the fundamental teachings of Islam. It includes basic concepts of faith, prayer, and moral values, aimed at fostering a strong foundation in Islamic belief.

Duration: 8 weeks
